About Me

A designer who codes,
a developer who designs

I've spent the last 8 years at the intersection of design and engineering. I believe the best digital products come from people who understand both sides — the pixel-perfect visual craft and the clean, maintainable code underneath.

Previously, I've worked with teams at Spotify, Shopify, and several early-stage startups. Today I focus on freelance product design and frontend development, helping companies build interfaces their users actually enjoy.

When I'm not designing or coding, you'll find me hiking the Pacific Northwest trails, experimenting with film photography, or trying to perfect my pour-over technique.
