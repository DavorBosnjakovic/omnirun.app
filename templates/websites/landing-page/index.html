FAQ

Common questions

Is there really a free plan?

Yes — Starter is free forever for up to 5 members and 3 projects. No credit card required. Upgrade to Pro whenever you need more capacity or advanced features.

Can I import from Trello, Asana, or Jira?

Absolutely. We have one-click importers for all three. Your boards, tasks, labels, and attachments come over automatically — usually in under 5 minutes.

What happens when my trial ends?

Your workspace drops back to Starter features. Nothing is deleted — you just lose access to Pro features until you upgrade. No surprises, no lock-in.

Is my data secure?

We're SOC 2 Type II certified. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Enterprise plans add SSO, SAML, and audit logging.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel with one click from your billing settings. You'll keep access through the end of your billing period. We don't do annual lock-ins unless you choose one for the discount.
