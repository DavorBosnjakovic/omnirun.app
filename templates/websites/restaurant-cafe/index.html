Our Story

Built around the fire

Ember & Oak started with a simple idea: bring people together around an open flame. In 2018, chef Maya Torres and partner Liam Beckett converted a century-old warehouse in Portland's Pearl District into a space where the kitchen is the centerpiece.

Everything we serve is cooked over wood fire or ember — no gas, no shortcuts. We source from farms within 50 miles, change the menu with the seasons, and treat every guest like a neighbor.

Seven years in, the fire still burns. Come pull up a chair.

Maya Torres Chef & Co-Founder
